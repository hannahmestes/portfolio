Platform: Mobile • XR • AI

Mobile-first immersive learning platform teaching job and language skills through storytelling. Designed to build confidence in healthcare, hospitality, and tech sectors without requiring VR headsets or classrooms.

SuperDoc
Company: Baltu Technologies
Platform: iPad

"On The Job Knowledge Platform" - Mobile instruction manual for capturing and sharing critical organizational knowledge. As easy as posting a selfie, SuperDoc prevents knowledge loss when employees leave.

Features step-by-step instructions with images, videos, and 3D models. Real-time content pushes ensure everyone has the latest version. Reduces human error and optimizes expert time.

Sundial
Company: State Farm RED Labs
Platform: iOS

Mobile app and Alexa skill helping older adults live independently while staying connected. Sundial enables aging in place by connecting seniors with their "Care Circle" of family and friends.

Led efforts to introduce an iPad version and build a custom mobile experience for the senior.

Features daily activities and reminders, photo sharing with personalized captions to Echo Show, care circle coordination, and check-ins. Simple interface designed for users who aren't tech-savvy.

Addresses demographic shift: by 2030, 1 in 5 U.S. residents will be retirement age, and 3 out of 4 older adults want to stay in their homes.
